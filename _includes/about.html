Feel free to contact through qianwen_wang@hms.harvard.edu

I am Qianwen Wang (汪倩雯), a PostDoc at Gehlenborgy Lab, Harvard University.
I obtained my PhD degree in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2020.
I got my B.Eng Degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2015. During my PhD study, I worked at the XAI Group， HKUST VisLab, under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu.
I have interned at Microsoft Research Asia and collaborate with researchers from MIT, University of Oxford, Tsinghua Univeristy, Microsoft Research.

I am studying Interactive Data Visualization within the broad context of Human-Computer Interaction, envisioning interactive data visualiation as an effective approach for knowledge communication with general users. My work strives to facilitate the communication between general users and complicated algorithms through creating interactive visual analysis systems.
