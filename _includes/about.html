I am Qianwen Wang (汪倩雯), a 5th-year PhD candidate in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am working at the XAI Group， HKUST VisLab, under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu. During my PhD studies, I have interned at Microsoft Research Asia and collaborated with researchers from MIT, Tsinghua Univeristy, Microsoft Research.

I am studying Interactive Data Visualization within the broad context of Human-Computer Interaction . My work strives to facilitate the communication between humans and Machine Learning Models through creating interactive visual analysis systems. Before joining HKUST, I got my B.Eng Degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2015.
